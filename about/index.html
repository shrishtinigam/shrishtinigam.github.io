About Me

I’m Meher, a software developer at BlackRock and an AI enthusiast focused on building scalable language model pipelines and high-performance data solutions.

My journey as a computer scientist started with a focus on AI and language modeling. During my undergraduate studies, I published two IEEE conference papers benchmarking BERT-based models for sentiment classification and evaluating decoder-only models, including Google’s PaLM, for NLP tasks. This early experience sparked my passion for LLMs, driving me to explore efficient model architectures, performance optimization, and real-world applications of large language models in both research and industry settings.

Performance engineering continues to be a central part of my work. I’ve delved into high-performance computing, SQL optimization, asynchronous programming in Python, and even written detailed guides on optimizing pandas workflows and async pipelines.

Before BlackRock, I interned at BMC Software on the Helix ITSM platform, building cloud-based automation plugins, scalable backend APIs, and lightweight polling services to improve performance and reduce server load. I also built a cloud-native microservices e-commerce app, experimenting with distributed systems, event-driven architectures, and end-to-end cloud deployment.

At BlackRock, I work in the Risk and Quantitative Analysis (RQA) Department, where I’m part of the Core Risk Engineering (CRE) Team. I build Python packages and SDKs to integrate with Aladdin APIs, modernize infrastructure, and streamline end-to-end ETL processes. I deploy Dockerized environments that span the entire data lifecycle — extracting from databases, messaging systems, RPC, FTP, and REST APIs; transforming data with Python and R; and delivering actionable insights through PowerBI and Python visualizations for Risk Managers. My work focuses on performance optimization, query efficiency, productionizing processes, and developing scalable, resilient, and maintainable low-code tools that empower Risk Managers across the firm.

Outside of work, I love to curate playlists. I have over 120 playlists across artists and moods. I love exploring new sounds, discovering hidden gems from underrated artists, so if you have any recommendations, do let me know!
